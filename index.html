12000 Words

Country to country, city to city, place to place,
I try to capture the essence of everywhere I go in a single 4x3 grid.
It's a challenge that forces me to make every shot count.
To make every moment memorable.
To discover.
To be unique.
To invoke emotion.
To convey more than I ever could through conventional language.
To make each picture worth so much more
— than just a thousand words.
